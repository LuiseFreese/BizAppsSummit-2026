# Model-Driven Apps for Canvas App Developers: Building Custom Pages with Enterprise Excellence - BizAppsSummit 2026

Welcome to the Model-Driven Apps for Canvas App Developers Powerclass! This tutorial is designed to empower canvas app developers as well as model-driven app developers by combining the best of both worlds: model-driven apps with custom pages - the perfect bridge between flexibility and enterprise-grade structure.

This comprehensive guide will take you through the basics of Dataverse and model-driven apps, but focuses on creating custom pages that seamlessly integrate into your apps, applying Fluent UI 2 principles for sleek, intuitive interfaces, and integrating AI to enhance user interactions.

## What You'll Learn

- Building custom pages that bring canvas-like flexibility into model-driven apps
- Applying Fluent UI 2 principles for sleek, intuitive interfaces  
- Seamlessly blending canvas and model-driven paradigms for robust enterprise solutions
- Creating responsive experiences across form factors
- Integrating AI prompts and automation to enhance user interactions
- A sneak peek into vibecoding with Generative Pages

## Prerequisites

**Technical Requirements:**
- Access to a Power Platform environment with Dataverse and premium licensing
  - We will provide temporary accounts for the workshop, OR
  - Bring your own Power Platform environment with appropriate licenses
- Basic familiarity with Power Apps (Canvas Apps)

**Hardware Requirements:**
- **Essential**: Laptop with charger
- **Recommended**: Second monitor/screen for better development experience
- **Optional**: External mouse for easier navigation

**Skill Level:**
You should have built at least a few Canvas Apps and be familiar with the main building blocks like galleries, forms, and controls. Experience with JavaScript is helpful but not required.
